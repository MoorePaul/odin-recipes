Lemon Vanilla French Toast

Description

French Toast with Berries on top. There is whipped cream available and a lot of sugar! If you want diabetes, this is the recipe for you! There will be no faster way to get you to the hospital, guaranteed!

Ingredients

  • bread
  • eggs
  • milk
  • sugar
  • berries (whichever you prefer)
  • nails (straight or screw)
  • corn syrup
  • epi-pen

Steps

  1. Stir eggs and milk together in a mixture
  2. Add entire bag of sugar to bowl and mix thoroughly
  3. Add mixture to pan and cook for 1 minute over medium heat
  4. Pour in the nails evenly
  5. Add the corn syrup to make sure you get diabetes and cancer
  6. I forgot to add the bread, so just throw it in somewhere here
  7. Take out and serve hot on a plate. Have epi-pen ready for first bite
