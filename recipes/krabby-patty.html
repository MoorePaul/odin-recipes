Krabby Patty

Description

The Krabby Patty is the Krusty Krab's signature food item. It is a meatless hamburger of sorts made from seaweed-sea buns, undersea vegetables, condiments, and a patty; it can also be ordered with cheese. The ingredients of the patty itself are purposely kept secret.
